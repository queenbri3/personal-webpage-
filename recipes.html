Spicy Honey Garlic Chicken
Bites

A perfect balance between sweet, spicy and savory!

Prep Time: 10 Minutes
Cook Time: 15 Minutes
Servings: 2

Ingredients:

- 2 boneless chicken breasts
- 2 tbsp cornstarch
- ½ tsp salt
- ½ tsp black pepper
- 1 tbsp oil (for frying)
- 2 tbsp butter
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tbsp honey
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tsp hot sauce (adjust to taste)
- ½ tsp red chili flakes (optional)
- 1 tsp sesame seeds (for garnish)
- 1 green onion, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

1. Coat the Chicken:
Toss chicken with cornstarch, salt, and pepper.

2. Cook the Chicken:
Heat oil and fry chicken for 5–7 mins until golden.

3. Make the Sauce:
Melt butter, sauté garlic, add honey, soy sauce, hot sauce, and chili flakes.

4. Combine:
Add cooked chicken back in and toss to coat.

5. Garnish & Serve:
Top with sesame seeds and green onions.

Nutrition information:

Calories: 350 kcal
Protein: 30g
Carbohydrates: 25g
Fat: 15g
Sugar: 15g
Fibre: 1g
